Balance Changes:

The Actually Additions solar panel has been made significantly cheaper to craft. It is now a viable early game power source.

The Immersive Engineering Windmill and Water Wheel have had their power output increased to better match the effort and space needed to set them up.

The Immersive Engineering Powered Lantern and Floodlight no longer require power to function. This was done for server performace reasons. However they are a bit more expensive to craft now.

Transmission loss has been removed from all Immersive Engineering wires.

Immersive Engineering wire coils, wire connectors, and wire relays are all much cheaper to craft.

Insulating Immersive Engineering wire coils is now slighly cheaper.

Creosote Oil can now be used to make various type of meat jerky.

The Immersive Engineering Blast Furnace is now easier to build.

The Immersive Engineering Blast Furnace can now make marble from limestone.

Some IC2 Macerator recipies have been duplicated to the IE Crusher and TE Pulverizer. This makes some non IC2 things producable outside of IC2.

IC2 Advanced Alloy can now be made in the IE Metal Press and TE Compactor. This allows for smoother advanced generator turbine progression without needing to use IC2.

The Engineer's Hammer can once again make plates from ingots.

Immersive Engineering fluid pipe is now much cheaper to craft.

Tar can now be melted into Creosote Oil.

Paper can now be made from Industrial Hemp.

Gold Plated Turbine Rotors are now craftable again.

The Tinkers Construct Crafting Station is back.

Immersive Engineering MV Wire Coils have had their maximum range increased to 24 blocks.

IC2 cables no longer melt when you try to put too much power through them.

IC2 machines no longer explode when you supply them with the wrong voltage.

The Resonanting Wand still doesn't detect Rock Crystal Ore (a bug in the mod). However Rock Crystal Ore is now much more common to compensate. Its still rare though.
